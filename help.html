How to play "Infinity Maze"

• Tap screen to move the ball in the corresponding direction.

• Tap "Launch a new game" to start a new game.

• Tap Easy, Medium, or Hard to change the level, which correspond to 5, 15, and 45 scores respectively.

• Subscribers get a special perk: double the scores.
